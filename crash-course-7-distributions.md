# Samples

instead of gathering data from entire populations, smaller samples are used to get a sense of the 'shape' of the visualized data which (by law of averages) shows the shape of the whole population.

## Distribution of data

- represents all possible values for a set of data and how often those values occur.
- Tells us about the shape and spread of data.

## normal distribution

- mean (average), median, and mode will be the same
- a standard bell curve
- mean tells us where the center of the distribution is
- standard deviation implies how wide or narrow the distribution is
    - standard deviation is the average distance from any point and the mean
