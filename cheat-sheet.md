## Multiplication Rule

- $ P(A \cap B) = P(B) \cdot P(B|A) $
- $ P(A|B) = \frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(B)} $
- $ P(B) = \frac{P(A \cap B)}{P(A|B)} $

## Addition Rule

- $ P(A \cup B) = P(A+B) - P(A \cap B) $
- $ P(A \cap B) = P(A+B) - P(A \cup B) $
